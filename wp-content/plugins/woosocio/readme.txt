Uploads/post your posts/products to Facebook wall, fan pages and groups on publish automatically. Also adds Facebook like box and like/share buttons for Woo products and posts.

== Description ==

<blockquote>
    <p>
        <strong>Post and Widget For Facebook</strong>
    </p>
</blockquote>

This is a simple plugin for your site to upload/post your products/posts to Facebook automatically. It will do the magic when you publish new post/product through WordPress. It will be shared to your Facebook friends, followers through your wall or pages and on your Facebook groups.
You can also add Facebook like box and like/share buttons to your woo products/posts and get more traffic.

== Installation ==

Here are few steps to follow:

1. Upload `woosocio` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Select WooSocio Logins page from woosocio menu and login.
4. That's it. You're ready to go!

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Great plugin! Can I share my post to my fan Page instead of my personal page? =

Yes. You can choose page (personal or fan) you want to share your posts from WooSocio Options page.

= Can I add a custom message to my post/product for sharing? =

Yes. You can add a custom message before publishing post/product. Click on edit link in publish area of the post.

= What if I don't want to share a post/product? =

If you uncheck the box 'Post to Facebook?' in publish area, it will not be shared.

= I am loggedin but posts/products are not uploading =

Make sure you gave proper rights to you application. Disconnect from woosocio logins and connect again.

= Can I allow more than one Facebook users to upload posts/products? =

Well, yes! more than one users can share your Facebook application for single site.

= What if I don't want like/share button on a post/product? =

If you uncheck the box 'show like/share button?' in publish area, like/share buttons will not be displayed.

= Do I need to connect or App ID to use like/share buttons? =

No. You can use like/share buttons without connecting to facebook. But it is recommended to get App ID from facebook and always connect to get insight stats and details.
